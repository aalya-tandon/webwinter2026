All about the MEMBERS:

Sophia Laforteza

Sophia Laforteza is a Filipino singer and dancer under Geffen Records and HYBE Labels. She is known for her role in the globla girl group KATSEYE, as their leader. In the survival show "The Debut: Dream Academy", she ranked 1st and qualified for debut, and then became an offical member of KATSEYE in 2024. She was born on December 31, 2002 in NYC, however she grew up in Manila, Phillippines. Her mother is a musical theater actress and singer, while her father is an executive chef. She is bilingual, speaking both Tagalog and English regularly. She has been singing and performing since age 5 with her mother as her mentor.

Daniela Avanzini

Daniela Avanzini is a Cuban/Venezuelan-American dancer, singer, and rapper under Geffen Records and HYBE Labels. She ranked 3rd in the survival show "The Debut:Dream Academy" and thus qualified to debut as a member of KATSEYE. Daniela was born on July 1, 2004 in Atlana, Georgia. Both her parents were professional Latin ballroom dancers, and thus Daniela was exposed to music and dance at a very young age. At just 9 years old, she made it to the Vegas Rounds of the eighth season of America's Got Talent as a samba dancer. Daniela is also bilingual, and speaks both English and Spanish fluently.

Lara Raj

Lara Raj is an American/Tamilian-Indian-Sri Lankan singer and dancer under Geffen Records and HYBE Labels. She is best known for her role in KATSEYE, where she ranked 2nd in the survival show "The Debut: Dream Academy". Lara was born on November 3, 2005 in Connecticut. Her mother is Indian and works as a Bollywood dance teacher. Her father is Indian-Sri Lankan and works as a businessman. Lara also has an older sister named Rhea who also writes and sings songs. Lara started performing at the age of 5 years old, and also worked alongside Michelle Obama in an ad about the Global Girls Alliance. Lara was actually the one who proposed the group's name.

Megan Skiendiel

Megan Skiendiel is a Chinese-American dancer, singer, model, and actress under Geffen Records and HYBE Labels. She ranked 5th in the survival show "The Debut:Dream Academy", and thus debuted in KATSEYE in 2024. Megan was born on Febuary 10, 2006 in Honolulu, Hawaii. Her mother is Chinese Singaporean and her father is Lithuanian, Swedish, German, Irish, Scottish, and English. Megan started her career very early, infact she started dancing at the age of one and started singing at age six. Before joining KATSEYE, Megan worked as a child actress and model. She had small roles, such as her roles in Sydney to the Max and Bosch:Legacy. Megan is also trilingual, and can speak English, Cantonese, and French fluently.

Manon Bannerman

Manon Bannerman is a Ghanaian/Swiss-Italian singer and model under Geffen Records and HYBE Labels. She is best known for her role in "The Debut:Dream Academy" survival show, where she ranked 6th and debuted in KATSEYE. Manon's full name is Meret Manon Sarpong Bannerman and she was born on June 26, 2002. She was born in Lucerne, Switzerland to a Swiss-Italian mother and a Ghanaian father. Manon always had the dream to perform onstage, and learnt the piano and ukulele at age 15, where she also started writing her own songs. Manon first gained popularity in 2020, through her TikTok and Instagram. She also worked with many brands, and by 2022 she had worked with PacSun, Gymshark, Hugo Boss, Charles & Keith, and Dermalogica. She also speaks Swiss German and English

Yoonchae Jeung

Yoonchae Jeung is a South Korean singer and dancer under Geffen Records and HYBE Labels. She is recognized for being in the survival show "The Debut:Dream Academy" and she placed 4th, thus qualifying for debut in KATSEYE. Yoonchae was born on December 6, 2007 in Seoul, South Korea. Both of Yoonchae's parents are from South Korea, and she has an older sister with whom she is very close. Before KATSEYE, Yoonchae was already a kpop trainee, and left that to join the survival show. Yoonchae is known as the "maknae" of the group, which means she is the youngest member, as she debuted at the age of 16. Yoonchae has already interacted with many other korean idols, such as members of the groups TWS, TxT, Illit, Enhypen, and more.
