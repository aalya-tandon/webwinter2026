All about the MEMBERS:

Roh Yun-ah

Roh Yunah (or Yunah) was born on January 15 in 2004. She is currently 22 years old, but has the Korean age of 24. She was born in Chungju, South Korea and trained for 5 years before competing in the survival show R U NEXT?. She is primarily known as the group's "mood maker" because of her energy and cheer. She has extremely versatile skills and a strong stage presence, which helps in both concerts and variety shows. Some fun facts about her include: She loves pink, she hates spicy food, she has a puppy named Louis, and her role model is Seulgi from Red Velvet.

Park Min-Ju

Park Minju (or Minju) was born on May 11 in 2004. She is currently 21 years old, but has the Korean age of 23-24. She was born in Namyangju, South Korea and started training at the age of 15 before competing in the survival show R U NEXT?. She is known for being introverted and loving. She is best known for her beautiful vocals and soft tone, and is deeply considered to be one of the group's visual members. Some fun facts about her include: Her nickname is Mandu, which means dumpling. She loves watching horror movies and hates mint chocolate.

Sakai Moka

Sakai Moka (or Moka) was born on October 8 in 2004. She was born in Fukuoka, Japan and was initially a trainee under HYBE Japan before joining ILLIT. Since she is Japanese, many Korean fans of ILLIT will address her using her Korean name, Kim Moka. She is seen as the Mother of the group, and is more timid and kind. Some fun facts about her include: She loves cats, her favorite season is summer, and she was introduced to K-POP by her mother, who was a big fan of BIGBANG. She also HATES bugs.

Lee Won-hee

Lee Wonhee ( or Wonhee) was born on June 26 in 2007. She was born in Busan, South Korea but later moved to Changwon. She competed on R U NEXT?, the survival show and ranked 1rst. Her audition song was "Hype Boy" to NewJeans. She is a timid and ambiguous person, but also active. Some fun facts about her include: She loves anything blueberry flavored, thus her favorite colors are sky blue and ivory. She was also scouted at an Express Bus Terminal Station, thus it is quite impressive that she got into ILLIT.

Hokazona Iroha

Hokazona Iroha (or Iroha) was born on February 4 in 2008. She is from Tokyo, Japan and was previously a trainee for JYP Entertainment. She is the youngest member of the group, and thus is called the maknae! Some fun facts about her include: She enjoys mint chocolate (which is twinning with Sunoo from ENHYPEN), and she loves wintertime. She likes to spend her days off sleeping and going for walks.
