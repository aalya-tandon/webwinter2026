HYBE's girl group ILLIT

Simple facts about ILLIT (See more on the ABOUT page):

  1. ILLIT debuted in 2024 under the parent entertainment company HYBE, and under the subgroup BE:LIFT

  2. Their debut song was called Magnetic and since then they have produced 50 official songs.

  3. They have a total of 5 different members, who all participated in the survival show R U NEXT?

  4. The group was initially supposed to have 6 members, but one member, Youngseo, left the group before their debut. She joined another group called ADP instead.

  5. Their fanbase is called GLITTS which is inspired by the word "glitter"

  6. The group has two Japanese members, Moka and Iroha, and three korean members, Wonhee, Minju, and Yunah.

  7. The group is supposed to have a dreamcore aesthetic, which is both beautiful and terrifying at the same time 😅
